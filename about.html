Who are we?

Lolhop Inc. is dedicated to uniting and entertaining gamers from all over the world. Lolhop Inc. was founded by a group of brilliant teenagers. Lolhop specializes in graphic art, programming, gaming, and film making. We will eventually have open lobbies in certain games. Lolhop founders would make appearances almost every day. Our channels offer hilarious content , and we will eventually offer authentic merchandise. The shop will include official Lolhop apparel, @lolhop.com email addresses, and access to premium Lolhop events, such as tournaments and exclusive servers. Lolhop Inc. is dedicated to uniting and entertaining the gamers of the world.

Videos

The Lolhop team pumps out many videos, from gameplay videos to action-packed shortfilms. All of our videos are produced and edited by Voltage Films.
